Equity Research

With a universe of about 15000 companies under coverage, we are the best equipped to provide you with an independent opinion on fair value. Our valuation represents the most likely price around which a deal can happen, if more than one independently acting potential buyer/seller is found after adequate efforts, but within a limited timeframe after our analysis. We offer full support in finding your way into the world of stock market.

Company Analysis

Our aim is to provide you with the status-quo of a company’s performance and enlightening our clients with its overall risk assessments, growth prospects, liquidity, key investment highlights, future prospects, business outlook dynamics, share price, performance analysis, key fundamentals and shareholder statistics, etc., thus helping you get the better picture of the company’s growth and making it easy for you to decide whether to invest or not.

Financial Reports

For you to make the smart investment choices, our team of experts work strenuously and methodically to provide you with the financial reports conveying the overall financial health of a company you’re planning to invest in, including the net sales turnover, stock adjustments, operating profit, prior year adjustments, EBITDA, reported PAT, reserves and surplus, ROA, ROE, IIEA, CTI, P/E, debt to equity ratio and more.
